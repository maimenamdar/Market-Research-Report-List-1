<p><p>The green roofing system market is categorized into three main types: intensive, extensive, and others. Intensive green roofs feature deeper soil layers, allowing for a wider variety of plants and accessibility for recreational use, but require more maintenance. Extensive green roofs have shallow soil layers, are lightweight, and predominantly use drought-resistant plants, making them easier to maintain. The "others" category includes hybrid systems and specialized applications like eco-roofs for specific environmental needs, reflecting diverse urban sustainability practices.</p></p>

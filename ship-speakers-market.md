<p><ul><li>Wall-Mount Type</li><li>Ceiling Mounted Type</li><li>Bar-Mounted Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ship speakers are categorized into three main market types based on installation styles: wall-mount type, ceiling-mounted type, and bar-mounted type. Wall-mount speakers are designed for vertical surfaces, providing clear sound in compact spaces. Ceiling-mounted speakers offer a discreet solution for enhancing audio without taking up floor space, ideal for larger areas. Bar-mounted speakers are typically installed along railings or ledges, delivering sound across wider areas while maintaining a streamlined design. Each type addresses specific spatial and acoustic needs on vessels.</p></p>

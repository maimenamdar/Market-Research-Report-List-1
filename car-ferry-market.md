<p><p>The Car Ferry market comprises two primary types: Monohull and Multihull. Monohull ferries have a single hull structure, offering stability and simplicity in design, making them suitable for shorter routes and calmer waters. In contrast, Multihull ferries feature two or more hulls, providing enhanced stability and greater capacity, ideal for longer distances and rougher seas. Both types serve critical roles in transporting vehicles and passengers across waterways, with each catering to specific operational needs and customer preferences.</p></p>

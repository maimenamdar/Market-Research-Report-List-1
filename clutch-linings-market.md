<p><p>The clutch linings market consists of various production methods, including calendering, glue application, and impregnation drying tower techniques. Calendering involves compressing materials to create uniform thickness, ensuring durability. The glue method uses adhesives to bond friction materials, enhancing performance and stability. Impregnation drying towers facilitate the infusion of resins into linings, improving heat resistance and wear properties. These types collectively contribute to the efficiency and reliability of clutch systems in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
